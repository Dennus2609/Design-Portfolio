Motion Graphics

tools

  • After effects
  • Premiere Pro
  • Blender
  • Figma

Visual storytelling and Interactive designs through motion graphics is the core of what I do. Designing simple animations that brings life to what was otherwise planned to be static and turning it into some sort of eye candy whether it be in a 2D screen or for in a virtual world is a joy that cannot be explained.

My collection of motion graphics i've done over the past couple of years has been through my works within the companies i've worked in as well as commissions and freelance gigs where i've led both the Video and audio experiences. So far, i've had the opportunity to collaborate with the likes of Flipkart, Nothing, Oppo and Cadbury, among others.

As an extension of my work on digital platforms i've had the opportunity to work and lead teams working on physical installations as part of temporary and permanent exhibitions in events and office buildings

But again, what better way to experience it than going through the full documentation.
