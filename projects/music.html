Music has been a constant companion throughout my life, from my early days of learning guitar to producing electronic music. What started as a hobby has evolved into a serious pursuit, with performances at various venues and events.

My journey in music production began with simple acoustic covers and gradually expanded into creating original compositions. I've experimented with different genres, from acoustic folk to electronic ambient music, always seeking to push my creative boundaries.

Through collaborations with other musicians and producers, I've had the opportunity to learn and grow, developing my own unique sound while contributing to various musical projects. The process of creating music has taught me valuable lessons about patience, creativity, and the importance of continuous learning.

Today, I continue to explore new musical territories, combining traditional instruments with modern production techniques to create immersive sonic experiences in my professional assignments as well my passion projects.
